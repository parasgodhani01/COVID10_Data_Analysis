About COVID-19 Data

Source of this data

country

The name of the country or region where the data is recorded.

continent

The continent where the country or region is located.

population

The population of the country or region.

day

The date on which the data was recorded, in the format dd-mm-yyyy.

time

The timestamp indicating the exact time the data was recorded.

Cases

The total number of confirmed COVID-19 cases reported in the country or region.

Recovered

The number of recovered COVID-19 cases in the country or region.

Deaths

The number of deaths caused by COVID-19 in the country or region.

Tests

The total number of COVID-19 tests conducted in the country or region.
